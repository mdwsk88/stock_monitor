# WeCom Group Announcement

This document provides an English version of the group announcement that can be posted directly in a WeCom group to show members how to use the robot properly.

## Recommended Group Announcement

```text
Hi everyone, the A-share research bot in this group is now connected to structured research tools. You can directly mention @Stock expert to ask questions.

Recommended prompts:
1. @Stock expert How does Kweichow Moutai look recently?
2. @Stock expert What recent bullish and bearish signals does Ping An Bank have?
3. @Stock expert Which stocks are worth watching today?
4. @Stock expert Which market direction is strongest today?
5. @Stock expert Which stocks are worth watching in the low-altitude economy theme?
6. @Stock expert Which stocks look risky today?
7. @Stock expert Why did Longquan appear in today’s evening report?
8. @Stock expert Is Zongshen Power a resonance name on today’s main market line?

The bot is especially good at:
- recent core events and bullish/bearish interpretation for a stock
- today’s market line and hot directions
- resonance opportunities between macro themes and stock notices
- recent high-risk or bearish stocks
- explaining why a stock appeared in today’s evening report

Usage tips:
- mention the stock name, short name, or ticker directly whenever possible
- for single-stock analysis, ask “How does XXX look recently?”
- for market ideas, ask “Which stocks are worth watching today?” or “Which direction is strongest today?”
- for a follow-up on a stock in the evening report, ask “Why did XXX appear in today’s evening report?”
- if you want the raw notice details, say “Show me the raw notices”

Please note:
- the bot provides event and signal analysis, not return promises
- if no high-value event is detected, the bot will say so directly
- the bot does not handle unrelated questions such as weather, coding, or life advice
- one topic per message usually gives the most stable answer
```
